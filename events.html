Hello, I am Cedric Pereira.

A part of my work over the years has been the facilitation and operation of a variety of events. These include promotional campaigns for storefronts, developers, publishers, and other groups that have been mutually beneficial to their interests and my own communities.
On top of that, I have also expanded the efforts of FreeGameFindings by creating a home for FGF on numerous platforms, expanding overall reach.

Events

Assistance In Product Launches, Sale Events, and Debuts of Games.

Large Out-Reach

The bulk of the events I have run, have been for the FreeGameFindings group that I made over a decade ago. Said group exists on many platforms, but the primary home houses over 350,000 subscribers, with tens of thousands of followers on other platforms. Platforms are Reddit for the main, with secondary locations such as Twitter, YouTube, TikTok, Instagram and more.

Event Posts

Plenty of our events have been promotional giveaway posts where the collaborative party supplies product(s) to be used as incentive to promote the collaborator in some way. These events often have multiple elements to them, but the main element is generally the landing page, or primary, post on the FGF group subreddit. Said posts get priority viewing where our thousands of active users can partake in and view the event. While there, again, can be other elements to these events, the main post for them generally garner at least 100,000 tracked views, which require a user opening and reading the thread.

Events like these always incorporate some sort of call to action for the users participating in them, resulting in promoting the collaborator's presence on platforms, or their games, etc. This is a mutual benefit as it also promotes our platform.

Video Content

Another aspect of FGF and the FGF collaborative events is the video content I produce. I create short-form videos to accommodate many posts for the FGF community. In terms of event videos, I have done a myriad of these as well.

Video content is created to be shared onto other platforms and encourage further growth of FGF outside of Reddit. YouTube, TikTok, Instagram and Twitter all house video content (and more) that can regularly bring in some comfortable views which has further grown the main platform.
